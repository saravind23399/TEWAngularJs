About The College Department of Mathematics
Mepco Schlenk Engineering College, Sivakasi is an ISO 9001:2015 certified, NACC accredited with 3.14 on a four point scale at “A” grade and an Autonomous Institution affiliated to Anna University, Chennai. The college was established in the year 1984 by Mepco Schlenk Charities founded by The Metal Powder Company Ltd., Tirumangalam, Madurai. The college is known for its excellence in infrastructure, teaching and research activities. The Institute offers 7 UG programs,which are accredited by NBA under Tier I category and 9 PG thprograms. The college has been sanctioned with 115 sponsored research projects worth of 13.00 crores. The College got 5 rank at National level in Top Engineering Colleges of th Eminence category in the CSR-GHRDC Engineering Colleges Survey June 2017 and 29 Rank among National Level Engineering Colleges including IITs, NITs, by Outlook Survey, June 2018.
The Department of Mathematics has striven to set high standards in teaching, right from the beginning by well-qualified faculty of fourteen members with eight Ph.D.'s and five members pursuing Ph.D. The department is currently having DST – SERB, NBHM funded projects. The department conducted many FDPs, STTPs, Workshops and Conferences Speakers
